bloody tentacles

you are a floating eyeball / jellyfish monster who hates helicopters and spikes.

control your body using WASD or arrow keys, and your tentacles with the mouse by mousing over things when the mouse button is down.

this game was made from scratch by thom chiovoloni in 72 hours as part of ludum dare 33. the theme was "you are the monster". for best results, please play using a recent version of firefox or chrome.

fps: 60 tps: 60 ms/f: 0.0160
